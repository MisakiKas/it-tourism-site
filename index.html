Explore the Historical Marvel of A Famosa

Discover the rich history and heritage of Malacca through the iconic fortress, A Famosa.

A Famosa is one of Malaysia's most iconic historical landmarks. Located in Malacca, this fortress was built by the Portuguese in the 16th century and remains a symbol of Malaysiaâ€™s colonial past.

Originally constructed in 1511, A Famosa served as a stronghold for the Portuguese, and later fell into the hands of the Dutch and British. Today, only the famous Porta de Santiago gate remains standing, offering a glimpse into the rich history of Malacca.

Visitors can explore the ruins, learn about the site's historical significance, and enjoy the surrounding attractions, including St. Paul's Church and the Malacca River.
